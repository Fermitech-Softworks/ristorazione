{{ gettext("What is Ristorazione?") }}

{{ gettext("Ristorazione is a freemium software that allows the owner of a restaurant to offer a rich experience to his customers, allowing them to choose their meal in a much more interactive and straightforward way, while at the same time reducing the use of paper menus, to reduce overall waste and making them more easily updatable.") }}

{{ gettext("What does \"freemium\" mean? ") }}

{{ gettext("The word freemium is the fusion of the words free and premium. This means, in regards to Ristorazione, that its essentials features are accessible by anyone, while some others are locked behind a monthly subscription. To learn more about this topic, please read the \"Subscription\" section.") }}

{{ gettext("How can Ristorazione help me with my restaurant?") }}

{{ gettext("Ristorazione allows restaurant owners to spend less money on their infrastructure. If you just need to host your menu, well, that's free. If you want to use Ristorazione as your order processing system, waiters no longer need expensive tablets or PDAs, but just their smartphones to collect the orders that the users submit (although it's recommended to have an internet-capable pc near the cash register to improve the usability of certain features). Ristorazione is also capable of handling take-aways and home delivery orders.") }}
